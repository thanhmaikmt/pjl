These are shots of the refrigerator door. You can clearly see a reflection of the rice cooker, which is behind the camera and to the right. It's a brushed stainless steel door, and does not make a clear reflection when viewed normally (with visible light, as in the third picture). But, it seems to act like a mirror for thermal radiation. I'm not sure why, but I assume it is because the wavelength of thermal radiation is too long to be scattered by the imperfections in the brushed steel surface.
