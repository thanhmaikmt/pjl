This applet can act as a high-pass filter by clicking the High-Pass Filter button. This removes low frequencies; pressing it multiple times filters out more frequencies. With low frequencies removed, only sharp or sudden changes in the function are left. Click the Play checkbox to hear the filtered function; without the low frequencies, it sounds tinny.
